My short story

Since I were young, I've always fascinated with computer and technology. I remembered that I'd spend all day playing around and clicking stuff. I set my mind back then that I want to work with a computer and create cool things in the future.

After finishing my High School in 2011, I went into Institute of Technology of Cambodia choosing a major in Computer Science. Then, in 4th year, I had an amazing opportunity to get a scholarship to pursue my study in France.

In 2015, I started my new student's life at Polytech Lille in a Department of Software Engineering and Statistics. For 2 years, through good and bad times, I successfully graduated with an Engineering's degree.

Soon after my graduation, I made an important decision to stay and find a job in France. It's for 2 good reasons: getting work experiences and challenging myself.

After 3 months of searching, I got an offer from one company as a Front End Developer. I am now looking forward to my first job and getting more experiences.
